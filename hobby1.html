Bowling

What is Bowling?

Bowling is a fun and competitive sport where players roll a heavy ball down a lane to knock over ten pins arranged in a triangle.

The game has 10 rounds (called frames), and players aim to get a strike (all pins down in one roll) or a spare (all pins down in two rolls). The maximum score in a perfect game is 300 points!

Bowling is a great hobby for people of all ages and is often played with friends, family, or in professional leagues.
